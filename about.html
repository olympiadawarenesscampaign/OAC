About OAC

The Olympiad Awareness Campaign (OAC) is a student-centric initiative designed to inform and guide learners about national and international Olympiads.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, resources, and mentorship that enhance problem-solving, critical thinking, and academic achievement through Olympiads.

What We Do

  • Explain various Olympiad exams and eligibility criteria.
  • Provide structured study plans by grade and subject.

  • Connect students with mentors and peer communities.
  • Ensure most resources are free or low-cost to maintain inclusion.

Vision

To make Olympiad participation accessible to all students and help build a generation of analytical thinkers and future innovators.
